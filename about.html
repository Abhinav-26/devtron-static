The Devtron

Devtron was established in 2019 by industry veterans with more than 15 years of experience under their belt. With its flagship platform - Release Orchestration and Application Lifecycle Management on Kubernetes - it is helping some of the most technologically advanced companies around the world to reduce their go to market time in a secure, stable and cost effective manner.

Our Mission

To democratise the adoption of best practices of DevOps through an Open Source Platform for Release Orchestration and Application Life Cycle Management on Kubernetes. Every technology enabled company on Kubernetes should be able to maintain the highest level of SLA/SLO for applications without costly upfront investment.

Why Devtron

Devtron automates Release Orchestration and Life Cycle Management of Applications running on Kubernetes. Its powerful features provide you complete Observability, Cost Optimization, Performance Optimization, Alerting, Monitoring and Debuggability without writing a single line of script. It has strong Policy Enforcement, RBAC and Audit Logs to ensure complete security.
