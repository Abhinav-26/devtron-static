Why we built devtron

Software delivery workflow DSLs are not domain aware. What's #Kubernetes, #BlueGreen, #FlakyTest?

Isolated software delivery and debugging platform. #ContextSwitching leads to #ProductivityLoss

Management of applications across environments is painful. #ConfigHell leads to #ConfigDrift

Enforcement and management of security policies is unwieldy. #SecurityPolicies debugging is #NotTopologyAware

Collaboration on helm charts across teams and environments is ineffective. #helmcharts are like #hydra, spawning two heads for every slain head

Agile processes are blindsided by missing devops metrics. #Measure before #ProcessImprovement

Almost impossible to achieve the optimal balance between Cost, Stability & Security.
